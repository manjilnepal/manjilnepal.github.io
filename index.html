About

I am a third-year Computer Science and Engineering student at SRM University-AP, Andhra Pradesh, India My research focuses on Deep Learning (DL) and Federated Learning (FL), with an emphasis on model architectures, training optimization, and system efficiency. Currently, I am working on FL research in the medical field, exploring privacy-preserving techniques for decentralized learning in healthcare applications.

I am passionate about advancing deep learning methodologies, optimizing model performance, and contributing to the evolving landscape of artificial intelligence, particularly at the intersection of FL, medical AI, and computer vision. I have a strong interest in Convolutional Neural Networks (CNNs) and Computer Vision. I actively implement research papers, coding and experimenting with different architectures to gain deeper insights into model behavior and advancements in the field. Recently, I have also been exploring Large Language Models (LLMs), studying their fine-tuning techniques and real-world applications.
